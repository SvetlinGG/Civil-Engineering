Golo Civil Engineering & Consulting Company

(GCECC) is a Somali Diaspora-led business consortium focused on construction, investment, affordable housing, agriculture, healthcare, sustainable tourism, and eco-development in Somalia. Comprising social entrepreneurs, investors, and professionals, they aim to promote investment opportunities for Somalia’s reconstruction and development.

About Golo

Established in 2007, (GCECC) is proud of its 13 years of achievement in the field of civil and related construction works. In the earlier years, the business activities in the last years its construction projects were expanded to Somalia.

During the recent years (GCECC) made joint ventures to expand the areas of expertise and has become one the established construction company in Somali outstanding Company. The reason for growth is expansion in its management structure, major investments in construction, equipment that helped in rising the annual turnover of the company.

During this period, (GCECC) delivered several projects; some of the key projects included are in Building and Villas in Mogadishu in the specialized fields and expanded business areas in water and wastewater treatment plants and information technology. Also established a metal fabrication workshops

With the professional, experienced & devoted workforce, possession of construction equipment and a progressive management the (GCECC) is moving towards a better future.
